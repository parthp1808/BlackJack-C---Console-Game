*************************
 UNDERAGE GAMING RESTRICTION 
*************************
Under the Gaming Control Act under the Ontario 
and Canadian legislation, there are rules and
regulations regarding the act of underage gaming. 
These restricti-ons are strictly enforced on any 
prohibited gaming activities performed by mino-rs. 
The minimum age to play a game of chance is 19 and 
penalties within Ontario range from $50,000 to 
$250000.
